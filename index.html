Woodcraft

Woodcraft is a Minecraft multiplayer server that seeks to effortlessly integrate various RPG aspects into the unique multiplayer experience. We have McMMO-based plugins that are specifically designed to interact together to provide players a really unique RPG experience. We also conduct a variety of server-wide events, such as building competitions and PVP competitions, in which users compete for rewards while populating the server with rich, vivid buildings and learning PVP strategy and tactics. Our community thrives on friendly interaction, and new players can expect to form friendships that will last beyond the server.
